# Wildfire

Fire behavior, planning, and community-facing wildfire research.

## Connected Projects

- [Extreme Wildfire](https://cu-esiil.github.io/ExtremeWildfire/): Collaborative research on extreme wildfire behavior and environmental change.
- [FIRE Plan 2024](https://cu-esiil.github.io/FIRE-plan-2024/): Planning and strategy work for fire management and shared response.
- [WUI Boundary](https://cu-esiil.github.io/WUI_boundary/): Measuring the wildland-urban interface across delineation and scale.
