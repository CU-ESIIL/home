# Synthesis & Collaboration

Team science, collaborative research design, and synthesis practice.

## Connected Projects

- [AI in a Day](https://cu-esiil.github.io/AI-in-a-day/): Cohort 3 working group exploring AI-enabled environmental synthesis and practical AI workflows for science.
- [Long Read Sequencing for Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi](https://cu-esiil.github.io/AMFungi_Biogeography_Diversity/): Using long-read sequencing to synthesize arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi biogeography and diversity.
- [CMSMapper](https://cu-esiil.github.io/CMSMapper/): Developing integrative strategies for siting climate mitigation solutions that balance nature, people, and land-use tradeoffs.
- [Ecosystems Under Invasion](https://cu-esiil.github.io/ecosystems-under-invasion/): Synthesizing how terrestrial ecosystem properties respond to degree of invasion.
- [EdgeSS: Sensor Synthesis at the Edge](https://cu-esiil.github.io/edgess/): Integrating real-time environmental sensor data, edge computing, and AI for scalable environmental insight.
- [Extreme Wildfire](https://cu-esiil.github.io/ExtremeWildfire/): Collaborative research on extreme wildfire behavior and environmental change.
- [Forest Carbon Codefest 2024 Group Sites](https://cu-esiil.github.io/home/event-groups/forest-carbon-codefest-2024/): Gallery of group websites created during the 2024 Forest Carbon Codefest.
- [Fungal Dispersal](https://cu-esiil.github.io/fungal_dispersal/): Open synthesis on fungal dispersal, connectivity, and ecological spread.
- [Hackathon 2023 Group Sites](https://cu-esiil.github.io/home/event-groups/hackathon-2023/): Gallery of team websites and datacube work created during the 2023 hackathon.
- [Innovation Summit 2024 Group Sites](https://cu-esiil.github.io/home/event-groups/innovation-summit-2024/): Gallery of participant-created team websites from the 2024 Innovation Summit.
- [Innovation Summit 2025 Group Sites](https://cu-esiil.github.io/home/event-groups/innovation-summit-2025/): Gallery of challenge-question group websites from the 2025 Innovation Summit.
- [Innovation Summit 2026 Group Sites](https://cu-esiil.github.io/home/event-groups/innovation-summit-2026/): Gallery of participant-created team websites from the 2026 Innovation Summit.
- [Macrophenology](https://cu-esiil.github.io/macrophenology/): Macroecological phenology patterns across changing environments.
- [NEON-SYNC](https://cu-esiil.github.io/NEON_SYNC/): Newly launched Cohort 3 working group site focused on NEON-SYNC; details and synthesis goals are in development.
- [OpenClaw Container](https://cu-esiil.github.io/openclaw_container/): A multi-agent scientific workspace combining repositories, files, AI assistants, and environmental data science workflows within a shared synthesis environment.
- [Spectral Diversity and Ecosystem Functioning](https://cu-esiil.github.io/spectral-diversity-ecosystem-functioning/): Exploring how spectral diversity can inform ecosystem function and environmental synthesis.
- [Team Science](https://cu-esiil.github.io/Team-Science/): Studying scientific collaboration networks and synthesis practice.
- [Thermo ToleRate](https://cu-esiil.github.io/Thermo-Tolerate/): Redefining ectotherm thermal tolerance under variable climate by accounting for temperature intensity and exposure duration.
- [Wopasi Pazo](https://cu-esiil.github.io/wopasi_pazo/): Newly launched Cohort 3 working group site; details and synthesis goals are in development.
- [Worldwide Plankton](https://cu-esiil.github.io/worldwideplankton/): Linking food web theory with global plankton datasets to quantify freshwater trophic interactions.
