# Data Infrastructure

Open infrastructure for containers, libraries, data access, and workflows.

## Connected Projects

- [AI for Natural Methane](https://cu-esiil.github.io/AI-for-Natural-Methane/): Harmonizing natural methane datasets and discovery workflows with AI.
- [Analytics Library](https://cu-esiil.github.io/analytics-library/): Data harmonization, workflows, and environmental analytics methods.
- [CMSMapper](https://cu-esiil.github.io/CMSMapper/): Developing integrative strategies for siting climate mitigation solutions that balance nature, people, and land-use tradeoffs.
- [CubeDynamics](https://cu-esiil.github.io/cubedynamics/): Composable operations for spatiotemporal data cubes.
- [Data Library](https://cu-esiil.github.io/data-library/): Organizational hub for datasets, discovery, and shared data access.
- [Datacube Sandbox](https://cu-esiil.github.io/Jim-s-Sandbox/): Training and experimentation space for data cube workflows.
- [EdgeSS: Sensor Synthesis at the Edge](https://cu-esiil.github.io/edgess/): Integrating real-time environmental sensor data, edge computing, and AI for scalable environmental insight.
- [Hermes Container](https://cu-esiil.github.io/hermes_container/): An AI-enabled scientific workspace providing integrated development environments, assistants, and reproducible research tools for environmental synthesis.
- [NEON-SYNC](https://cu-esiil.github.io/NEON_SYNC/): Newly launched Cohort 3 working group site focused on NEON-SYNC; details and synthesis goals are in development.
- [OpenClaw Container](https://cu-esiil.github.io/openclaw_container/): A multi-agent scientific workspace combining repositories, files, AI assistants, and environmental data science workflows within a shared synthesis environment.
- [Train-the-Trainer Materials](https://cu-esiil.github.io/home/trainings/): Central training page for technical trainings and onboarding materials.
