# Environmental AI

Machine learning and AI methods applied to environmental questions.

## Connected Projects

- [AI for Natural Methane](https://cu-esiil.github.io/AI-for-Natural-Methane/): Harmonizing natural methane datasets and discovery workflows with AI.
- [AI in a Day](https://cu-esiil.github.io/AI-in-a-day/): Cohort 3 working group exploring AI-enabled environmental synthesis and practical AI workflows for science.
- [EdgeSS: Sensor Synthesis at the Edge](https://cu-esiil.github.io/edgess/): Integrating real-time environmental sensor data, edge computing, and AI for scalable environmental insight.
- [HDR-ML Hackathon 2026](https://github.com/CU-ESIIL/HDR-ML-Hackathon-2026): Hands-on machine learning collaboration around environmental datasets.
- [Hermes Container](https://cu-esiil.github.io/hermes_container/): An AI-enabled scientific workspace providing integrated development environments, assistants, and reproducible research tools for environmental synthesis.
- [Innovation Summit 2026](https://cu-esiil.github.io/Innovation-Summit-2026/): AI for sustainability and environmental data-to-decision exchange.
- [OpenClaw Container](https://cu-esiil.github.io/openclaw_container/): A multi-agent scientific workspace combining repositories, files, AI assistants, and environmental data science workflows within a shared synthesis environment.
- [Spectral Diversity and Ecosystem Functioning](https://cu-esiil.github.io/spectral-diversity-ecosystem-functioning/): Exploring how spectral diversity can inform ecosystem function and environmental synthesis.
